NIJISANJI Vtuber Shu Yamino

Shu Yamino is apart of a corporate Virtual Youtuber company called Nijisanji. He debuted on December 21, 2021 under Nijisanji English branch's
5th wave Luxiem. He is apart of the first English male wave in the company.
Luxiem consists of 5 members: Shu Yamino, Vox Akuma, Luca Kaneshiro, Mysta Rias, and Ike Eveland.
